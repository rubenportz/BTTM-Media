Frequently Asked Questions

How does pricing work?

We charge a monthly retainer fee ranging from £1500-£3000. The retainer fee is calculated based on the number of hours our team needs to put into your website to achieve results. We amortise these hours into the monthly retainer fee. The fees are due at the beginning of each month to retain our agency. However, we usually work with 6 month retainers as that's approximately the time it takes to get results.

How long does it take to see results?

"Result" are heavily dependent on your goal and sector. For an indication it would be best to get in touch with one of our executives. As a figure to keep in mind, it takes at least 100 days to analyse a new content campaign exlcuding time to readjust and optimise. Anyone who guarantee shorter performance cycles is not taking their job responsibly.

Do you guarantee results?

We DON'T guarantee results. Although some agencies might approach with guarantees, please be very cautious as this indicates a lack of experience. The end goal is to rank your website higher in search engines. This involves optimising for a significant complicated search algorithm which changes almost on a daily basis with long-term internal and external factors as competitors, authority and age. Although we don't guarantee results, we do guarantee to put your website in its best shape according to the latest SEO best practices.

What's included in your SEO service?

Our SEO service consists of a robust audit, content and keyword plan, analytics reporting and continuous optimisation. Since these big terms entail quite a lot - it's easier to tell you what we DON'T do. In our standard SEO package we do execute on a link building strategy, we DON'T include the link acquisition within the retainer fee. While we handle the link acquisition for almost all clients, we ask for a small budget ensuring we can buy quality links or write quality content. Regarding content, we do provide a thorough content plan, but we DON'T produce the content within the retainer fee. We're more than happy to work with your writers or ask for a small budget paying for our contracted content writers. As we do not have expert writers in every industry, we will contract and manage them or work closely with your team to ensure quality and industry specific content.
